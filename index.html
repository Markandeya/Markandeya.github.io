What is Yamistha

The CET School of Management is bringing back the old tales with new twists and turns with the new edition of 'Yamistha 2020'. This year it's not just about workshops and competitions, it's about awakening the innovation and creativity within you.

With the addition of Startup Street with more than 100 startups lining up and along with Internship Fair for both management and technical students, Yamistha is arranging a basket full of goodies for you to grab! Make sure you take the most of it on Jan 10th - 12th 2020.
